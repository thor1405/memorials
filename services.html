Our Comprehensive Services

When you call Gomes Funeral Service, you can be assured of receiving comprehensive and professional services. We will work closely with you to create a personalized experience that honors your loved one's life. Our commitment is to provide dignified farewells that reflect the unique journey of each individual across Goa.

Embalming & Dressing

Our experienced staff will ensure your loved one is prepared with care, dignity, and utmost respect, following all religious, traditional, and cultural guidelines.

Funeral Attire & Accessories

We provide a range of dignified funeral attire and accessories, including veils, crosses, shoes, coffin stands, and lowering machines, catering to traditional Christian ceremonies.

Coffin Selection

Choose from our extensive collection of high-quality handcrafted coffins, including carved solid hardwoods, rosewood veneers, and marble composites, to find the perfect resting place.

Hearse Van & Mobile Morgue

We provide state-of-the-art mobile morgue refrigeration equipment and climate-controlled express hearse vans to ensure a dignified service across Goa with complete reliability.

Floral Arrangements

Our master florists craft breathtaking floral tributes, including custom memorial wreaths, casket sprays, white lilies, church altar arrangements, and sanctuary displays.

Catering Contacts

We connect families with trusted memorial catering partners across Goa and coordinate dignified refreshment and reception services for family, mourners, and guests.

Haven of Serenity
