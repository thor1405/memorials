Our Comprehensive Services

When you call Gomes Funeral Service, you can be assured of receiving comprehensive and professional services. We will work closely with you to create a personalized experience that honors your loved one's life. Our commitment is to provide dignified farewells that reflect the unique journey of each individual across Goa.

Funeral Accessories

We provide a range of dignified funeral accessories, including white bed sheet, crosses, shoes, coffin stands catering to traditional Christian ceremonies.

Coffin Selection

Choose from our extensive collection of high-quality handcrafted coffins, including sturdy plywood, solid hardwood, and polished wood veneers, to find the perfect resting place.

Hearse Van

We provide dignified, express hearse vans across Goa with experienced drivers to ensure reliable transport with complete respect.
