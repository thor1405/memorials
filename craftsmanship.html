Craftsmanship & Process

From ethical forest harvesting to white-glove sanctuary transport, Gomes Funeral Service ensures uncompromising quality, sacred reverence, and meticulous detail in every coffin we build across Goa.

Step 01 — Sustainable Material Sourcing

Hand-Selected Plywood & Hardwoods

We ethically source high-grade Goan plywood, premium solid hardwoods, and lustrous wood veneers, inspecting every grain for strength and eternal beauty.

Step 02 — Master Woodworking & Assembly

Artisanal Sculpting & Velvet Interiors

Master craftsmen assemble solid bronze swing bars, precision joinery, and hand-tailored champagne crepe and white velvet resting interiors.

White-Glove Hearse Transport

We provide dedicated, climate-controlled express transport directly to your church, sanctuary, or memorial residence across Goa.
